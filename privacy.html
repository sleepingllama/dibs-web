Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Dibs is built for households. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work, and we never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following when you use Dibs:

  • Account info: email address and display name used to create your account
  • Household data: chores, assignments, schedules, and message board posts shared within your household
  • Device info: push notification tokens to deliver reminders to your device
  • Usage data: crash reports and basic analytics to improve app stability

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and sync your household data across devices
  • To send push notifications and daily summary reminders you configure
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Firebase/Google as our infrastructure provider
  • With Crashlytics for crash reporting
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Household Members

When you join or create a household, your display name and chore activity are visible to other members of that household. Leaving a household removes your active membership but historical chore records remain for continuity.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@dibs.app. Account deletion removes your personal information within 30 days.

7. Children

Dibs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: support@dibs.app.
